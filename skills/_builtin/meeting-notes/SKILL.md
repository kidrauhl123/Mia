# Meeting Notes

Use this skill for meeting transcripts, rough notes, interviews, standups,
project reviews, brainstorms, and discussion records that need to become
structured minutes or action items.

## Workflow

1. Identify meeting type, participants if available, and the expected format.
2. Group content by agenda or topic instead of raw speaking order.
3. Separate decisions, action items, risks, blockers, and open questions.
4. Assign owners and due dates only when the source states them. Otherwise mark
   them as "待确认".
5. Keep disagreements and unresolved options visible when they matter.

## Output

- Provide a short executive summary first.
- Use a table for action items: task, owner, due date, status.
- Do not turn speculation into decisions.
