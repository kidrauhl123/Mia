# Paper Research

Use this skill when helping with papers, reading lists, literature reviews,
开题, citations, or research-note synthesis.

## Workflow

1. Clarify the research topic, course or paper goal, and required output.
2. Inventory the provided files or references before drawing conclusions.
3. Extract each source into comparable fields: author/year, question, method,
   dataset/material, conclusion, limitation, and useful quote or page anchor.
4. Group sources by theme or method, not just by upload order.
5. Produce a table first when there are 3+ sources, then summarize patterns.

## Output

- Keep claims tied to specific papers when possible.
- Mark uncertain or missing metadata instead of inventing it.
- End with 2-4 concrete next writing steps.
